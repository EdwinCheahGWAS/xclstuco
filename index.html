XWA Student Council

Meet the changemakers of the XWA school community

Who are the Student Council?

The Student Council are an entirely student-led organisation that strives for improvements within the school community. By supporting the pillars of arts, academics, sports, and service, we function in different departments to engage with students with all different kinds of events! Our members consist of students all over SY who excel at what they do, sharing their skills and experience to enhance the school community.

Student Council's Vision:



To enhance the school community and connect with students
