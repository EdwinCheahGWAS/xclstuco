Movember

The XWA Student Council organised a fundraiser for the Movember foundation advocating for men's health. By setting up a photo booth and 'Pie in the Face' stations, we were able to raise $518 in total! Students had a lot of fun smudging shaving cream on their teachers' and president's faces for a good cause!
